Consultancy Services

In addition to disease management, our consultancy services focus on nutrition optimization. We understand that a well-balanced diet is essential for the growth, immune response, and productivity of poultry. Our consultants analyze the nutritional requirements of each flock, considering factors such as age, breed, and production goals. Based on this assessment, we develop customized feeding programs and recommend high-quality feed supplements to ensure that the nutritional needs of the flock are met effectively. Flock management is another crucial aspect we address. Our consultants work closely with farmers to optimize flock management strategies, encompassing various factors such as housing, ventilation, lighting, and biosecurity measures. We provide guidance on maintaining optimal flock size, improving feed conversion ratios, implementing biosecurity protocols, and enhancing overall productivity while upholding animal welfare standards. At Pragati Agrotech India, we are committed to empowering poultry farmers with the knowledge and tools they need to succeed. Our consultancy services are designed to address specific challenges, deliver practical solutions, and offer ongoing support to ensure the long-term success of our clients' poultry operations.
